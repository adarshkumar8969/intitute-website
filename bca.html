Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA)

Best BCA College in Patna - Our BCA courses is aimed at helping students gain a proper understanding and sound knowledge of computer applications by the time they graduate. This becomes the foundation for pursuing further interests either in higher education or for entering the workplace in the vast, lucrative arena of Information Technology (IT).

Our course covers in-depth study of diverse subject categories, from programming basics and computer fundamentals to database and
application packages, networking and internet, logical and numerical methods. The development of core competencies in computer applications ensures that our course prepares the groundwork for grooming some of the best talent for the
I.T industry.

job Opportunities

BCA degree lets students opt for work in a multitude of capacities, which include, but are not limited to: Software Programmer, Multimedia/Web Programmer, Web Designer , Database Administrator, System and Network Administrator, or as a member of the Faculty for Computer Science/Computer Application Technologies in case the student has aspirations to teach.

Pre-requisite for Admission

Any student who has passed 10 + 2 in any discipline with a minimum of 45% marks (SC/ST/EBC/PH students 40%) is eligible for admission. The medium of instruction as well as examination is English. All students are required to undergo an English Proficiency Test post admission. Based on their performance in this test, they will be divided into different groups for English Proficiency Classes.
