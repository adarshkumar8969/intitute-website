To cater to the vast managerial need of the country today, our course curriculum is equipped to be a great combination of theoretical and practical knowledge of the business world. Pre-requisite for Admission

Any student who has passed 10 + 2 in any discipline with a minimum of 45% marks (SC/ST/EBC/PH students 40%) is eligible for admission. The medium of instruction as well as examination is English. All students are required to undergo an English Proficiency Test post admission. Based on their performance in this test, they will be divided into different groups for English Proficiency Classes.

on job TRANING

Students will undergo a rigorous On the Job Training (O.J.T) at the end of the fourth semester for about four weeks. The institution endeavors to find opportunities outside Patna for such periods to provide them with more all-round exposure. All the expenses of such training will be met by the students. They will also undergo another OJT of a shorter period in the Service sector which would mostly be local.
