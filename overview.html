OVERVIEW

Advance directional institute(ADinstitute), affiliated to Aryabhatta Knowledge University, Patna was founded by the group leaders in 2014 and has been carving out a niche for itself as the leading institution in Bihar in terms of imparting quality education to undergraduate students, pursuing Bachelor in Business Management (BBA), Bachelor in Computer Application (BCA) , Bachelor in Commerce (Professional) (BCP) and Bachelor in Mass Communication( BMC).

AD institute aims at producing industry-ready professional, focusing on providing theoretical knowledge to students as well as training them for jobs that are based on practical activities. The institution continually is evolving to meet its goal in ever changing business environment.

Besides, offering the best in academics AD instituteencourages an overall development of the students through various extracurricular activities conducted and taught by talented, experienced and capable faculty members.
